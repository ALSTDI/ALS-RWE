# :material-account-group: Weekly Community Meeting

The OHDSI/OMOP Train-the-Trainer community meets weekly to share progress, work through questions, and build skills together. All participants in the current cohort are encouraged to attend. Sessions are recorded and shared in the program repository.

---

## Meeting Details

| Item | Details |
|:--|:--|
| **When** | Fridays, 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM (Eastern) |
| **Format** | Video call (Zoom / Teams — see your cohort's communication channel for link) |
| **Recording** | Posted to the shared drive within 24 hours |
| **Notes** | Shared document linked in your cohort's channel |

!!! info "Link and calendar invite"
    The Zoom / Teams link and calendar invite are distributed by the program coordinator at the start of each cohort. Contact your site's program lead if you haven't received it.

---

## Typical Meeting Agenda

| Time | Item |
|:--|:--|
| 0:00 – 0:05 | Welcome and attendance |
| 0:05 – 0:20 | Recap: what we covered last session |
| 0:20 – 0:40 | Participant questions and working through blockers |
| 0:40 – 0:50 | Preview: what's coming next session |
| 0:50 – 1:00 | Open discussion, announcements, and wrap-up |

---

## How to Get the Most Out of the Meeting

!!! tip "Come with a question"
    The most productive meetings happen when participants arrive with a specific blocker or question — "I tried Step 3 of the Day 2 exercise and got a different count than Atlas." Bring a screenshot or the SQL you ran.

- **Review the previous session's module** before the call so questions are concrete.
- **Post questions in the channel beforehand** — the facilitator can prepare examples or look up answers in advance.
- **Share your screen** if you hit a confusing result. Live troubleshooting is often the fastest way to learn.
- **Trainers:** update the running notes document during the call so questions and answers are captured for participants who miss a session.
